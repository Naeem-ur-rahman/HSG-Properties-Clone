EJARI REGISTRATION

As per the RERA Regulations, each Tenancy Contract in Dubai is to be registered with Ejari. Its major aim is to support tenants, landlords and management companies. It benefits tenants by providing them a central and authorized platform, where they can get their tenancy contracts and rents regulated. HSG Properties can register your Tenancy for you. Just ask your Consultant to find out the procedure.

FREE PROPERTY VALUATION

HSG Properties knowns very well that owning a property is a big decision. But on the other side, selling a property with a premium is the other half of the mission. We have a dedicated team to assess your property and provide you with a realistic valuation taking into consideration a checklist of the current market, property purchase price, location, owner's expectation and forecast and many others. Don't hesitate to ask your Consultant for a Property Valuation free of charge to help you take the right decision at the right time.
